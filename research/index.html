Research

Overview of my main research directions and projects. See Publications for the full list of papers.

GPTAnno: Ontology-guided LLM for cell type annotation

Cell Ontology (CL) referenced framework for automated single-cell RNA-seq cell type annotation, with hierarchical subclustering and composite scoring. We benchmark across diverse datasets and species; the tool outperforms GPTCelltype, SingleR, AzimuthAPI, and popV. Released as an open-source R package.

Paper (bioRxiv)

Charting postnatal heart development

Integration of 400k+ single-nucleus RNA-seq with Xenium spatial transcriptomics across postnatal time points to build a temporal-spatial atlas of mouse heart development. We map cellular niches, identify co-expression modules and gene maturation scores validated by ablation studies, and provide an interactive spatial atlas.

Paper (bioRxiv)

HR-VILAGE-3K3M: Respiratory viral immunization gene expression

Largest multi-platform respiratory viral immunization dataset (14,136 profiles from 3,178 subjects across 66 studies). Curated for QC, batch correction, and vaccine response prediction. Released as an open-access resource on Hugging Face for method benchmarking.

Paper (arXiv) · Hugging Face

Single-cell dual omics of cardiac aging

Single-cell RNA and ATAC of non-cardiomyocytes in the aging mouse heart to characterize transcriptomic and epigenomic diversity and cell-type-specific aging phenotypes. We infer gene regulatory networks and cell–cell communication and identify aging-responsive subclusters.

Paper (iScience)
