bioRxiv, 2025

Charting Postnatal Heart Development Using In Vivo Single-Cell Functional Genomics

Haofei Wang, Yan Dong, Yiran Song, Marisol Colon, Nathan Yapundich, Samuel Ricketts, Xizi Liu, Jie Liu, Li Qian, et al.

bioRxiv, 2025

Integrated 400k+ single-nucleus RNA-seq with Xenium spatial transcriptomics across postnatal time points, constructing a temporal-spatial atlas of mouse heart development; mapped cellular niches and gene module maturation scores validated by ablation studies.

arXiv, 2025
